DOM Hell

Game Rules

  • Use the arrow keys to move.
  • Avoid incoming projectiles.
  • Collect green collectibles for points.
  • Lose a life when hit by a projectile (3 lives total).
  • Last player standing or highest score wins.
  • Duration: 2 minutes.

Controls

  • Arrow keys for movement.
  • Menu button to pause/resume/restart/exit.
